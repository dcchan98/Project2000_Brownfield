About Us

Equipped with years of experience and expertise in the industry, Projects 2000 specializes in the reinstatement of commercial property as well as demolition work for landed properties, commercial offices, retail stores as well as resorts/hotels. We pride ourselves for our delivery of timely and excellent quality work. Our capable team of interior designers, contractors and staff will assist you for every step of your interior design, renovation and decoration process to provide the best customer service and satisfaction. Projects 2000 has had success in executing high-end project works that includes Four Season Hotel and various resorts in Batam, and we are working on achieving greater heights and accomplishments for our humble company.
